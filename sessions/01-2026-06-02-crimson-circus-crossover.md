Also offered/detailed by the DM:
- **Potion of Watchful Rest** — complete a long rest while semi-aware; advantage
  on Perception to notice danger during the rest; can't be magically slept; not
  surprised unless the enemy is especially hidden/magical.
- **Greater Antitoxin** — for 1 hour: advantage on saves vs poison, resistance
  to poison damage, advantage on saves vs **lycanthropy infection from bites**,
  and a reroll on the first failed save vs curse/venom/wolf bite.
- **Black Salt** (10 gp/pouch, 3 uses) — pour a 5-ft protective circle (undead,
  fiends, shadow creatures attack at disadvantage); sprinkle to reveal ground
  touched by undead/fiendish/shadow magic in the last 24h (salt turns gray and
  hisses); thrown at a shadow creature within 10 ft, DC 12 Con save or it can't
  turn invisible/hide in shadows/teleport through darkness until end of its next
  turn.
- **Glass Eye Charm** (35 gp) — 1/day, bonus action: for 10 minutes gain
  advantage on one of Perception (spot hidden creatures), Investigation (secret
  doors/traps/hidden objects), or Insight (detect lies); dulls until next dawn
  after one use.
