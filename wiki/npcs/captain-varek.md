# Captain Varek

A **Black Banner** officer who commanded the patrol guarding the surface of the
excavation site above the buried archive. The party recovered his papers and
gear after the encounter.

## What we know
- Held the rank of **Captain** and reported directly to **Commander S. Valane**. (`wiki/documents/captain-vareks-papers.md`)
- Standing orders: hold the surface, admit no civilians, treasure-hunters, or local authorities, and secure any surviving records — recovering documents over saving lives. (`wiki/documents/captain-vareks-papers.md`)
- Carried a **Black Banner signet ring** and a **Solamnic officer's compass** (how he came by the compass is unknown — looted, inherited, or his own). (`wiki/documents/captain-vareks-papers.md`)
- His field journal logged a denied extraction request from "Survey Team Three" and rumors of a "Marshal" below, then cut off mid-sentence. (`wiki/documents/captain-vareks-papers.md`)
