# Pellan Voss

## What we know

- The **human ("the other person")** leading the [Seeker](../factions/the-seekers.md)
  roadblock crew at Solace, alongside the goblins. Wielded a **serviceable rapier.**
  (Session 03)
- Took orders from **[Fewmaster Toede](fewmaster-toede.md)**, who told him to "stop
  asking questions and watch the road" and to intercept the woman with the staff —
  i.e., **[Goldmoon](goldmoon.md)** and **[Riverwind](riverwind.md)**.
  ([Seeker Field Order — Toede to Voss](../documents/seeker-field-order-toede.md))
- **Killed**, with the rest of the roadblock crew, when the search turned to combat.
  (Session 03)
