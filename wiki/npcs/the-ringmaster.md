## What we know
- Runs a traveling circus and keeps a detailed ledger; signs notes "— The Ringmaster". ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Back-Cover List](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-back-cover-list.md))
- Smuggles "special cargo" in a locked wagon, timed to move "best before war." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Special Cargo / Things Not To Say](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-special-cargo.md))
- Traffics in "miracle-bearers," sold "breathing," and warns against revealing them — "no blue light, no singing under the floorboards." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Special Cargo / Things Not To Say](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-special-cargo.md))
- Keeps his own performers ignorant of the ledger's contents. ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Special Cargo / Things Not To Say](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-special-cargo.md))
- Has lied to the old knight, telling him the wagons "went west." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Special Cargo / Things Not To Say](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-special-cargo.md))
- Expects "the Queen does not need my circus once the armies march." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Back-Cover List](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-back-cover-list.md))
- Wary of hidden powers: "even stages have masters in the dark." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Back-Cover List](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-back-cover-list.md))
