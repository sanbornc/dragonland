# The Lute Wizard

An **unnamed wizard** the party encountered in the **[Inn of the Last Home](../locations/inn-of-the-last-home.md)**
in [Solace](../locations/solace.md) (Session 04). Despite no name yet, his actions
mark him as a **knowing player** in the Staff story, not a random patron.

## What we know

- He is a **wizard** among the inn's patrons. (Session 04)
- He **produced a lute and invited [Goldmoon](goldmoon.md) to sing** — and she sang
  **[The Song of Riverwind](../documents/song-of-riverwind.md)**, the ballad of her own
  story and the blue [Staff](../items/the-staff.md). (Session 04)
- When [Hederick](high-theocrat-hederick.md) fell into the fire, **he handed
  [Boilerdang](../characters/boilerdang-criddlycrotch.md) the [Staff](../items/the-staff.md)** —
  **Goldmoon's Staff** — with which Boilerdang **healed** the High Theocrat's burns.
  (Session 04)
- So he **knew what the Staff was, knew it could heal, and had it to hand** at the
  decisive moment. He appears to be **steering events** — choosing the song, supplying
  the relic. (Session 04)
- **He did not get the Staff from Goldmoon willingly.** It does **not** appear she
  consented to him taking/using it — so he is **not simply her agent.** He either took
  it, palmed it, or otherwise obtained it without her agreement, which makes his motives
  ambiguous: protector, manipulator, or someone with his own claim on the relic.
  (Session 04)

## Open questions

- **Who is he, and what is his name?**
- How does he **know Goldmoon** (well enough to call her up to sing) and **the Staff**
  (well enough to wield it as the answer)? How did he come to be holding it?
- Is he an **ally** (protecting Goldmoon, guiding the party), a **guide/mentor** figure,
  or something stranger? Was the whole sequence — song, fire, healing — engineered?
- Does he know more about the Staff's nature and origin than anyone the party has met?
