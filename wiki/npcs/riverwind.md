# Riverwind

## What we know

- The **man** of the two travelers the party rescued from the bears and is now
  **escorting to [Solace](../locations/solace.md).** (Session 03)
- A **"tall plainsman."** Travels with **[Goldmoon](goldmoon.md)**, the Que-Shu
  "Chieftain's daughter." (Session 03,
  [intercept orders](../documents/seeker-field-order-toede.md))
- The Seekers treat him as **secondary to the woman/staff** — their orders call him
  "expendable" and say he "may be subdued by force if necessary," while Goldmoon is
  to be taken **alive.** (Session 03)
- For the **shared story** (the rescue, the escort, seeking Hederick), see
  [The Two Travelers](the-two-travelers.md).
