# Sir Garreck

A spectral knight haunting the tower the party is exploring. He did not realize
he was dead until the party convinced him; now lucid, he is leading them down
through the tower.

## What we know
- A **ghost / undead knight** bound to the tower; he was unaware of his own death until the party persuaded him of it. (session 02)
- Once convinced, he turned cooperative and is **guiding the party down** through the tower's lower levels. (session 02)
