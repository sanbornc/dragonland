# The Staff & Goldmoon

**Status: active.** The party is escorting [Goldmoon](../npcs/goldmoon.md), who
unknowingly-to-the-Seekers still carries the long-sought **[Staff](../items/the-staff.md)**,
into [Solace](../locations/solace.md) — the very town from which
[Fewmaster Toede](../npcs/fewmaster-toede.md) is running a hunt to seize it.

## The thread
- The campaign has circled a **Staff** for some time: per the Ringmaster's ledger, **"the Staff must not reach [X.T.](../locations/x-t.md)"** — the buyer said it twice. ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Torn Map Scrap](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-map-scrap.md))
- **Goldmoon carries that Staff**, wrapped in cloth or leather. She is a **Que-Shu** woman, "the Chieftain's daughter," traveling with the plainsman **[Riverwind](../npcs/riverwind.md)**. (Session 03)
- The party rescued the pair from goblin-wounded bears in the forest and **agreed to escort them to Solace.** (Session 03)
- The Solace roadblock was a **targeted ambush**: standing orders were to **detain the woman alive, seize the Staff intact**, treat Riverwind as "expendable," and **keep priests, scholars, and Solamnic sympathizers away** from the object — run by **Toede in Solace, "by authority of the High Theocrat's office."** ([Seeker Intercept Orders](../documents/seeker-field-order-toede.md))
- The party **wiped out the roadblock crew** and **staged the scene as internal infighting** (successful Deception), so Toede should not yet suspect them. (Session 03)

## Open questions
- **What is the Staff, and what does it do** — and why must it be kept from X.T.?
- **How did Goldmoon come to carry it,** and does she know its full significance or that she's hunted for it?
- Do the party **tell Goldmoon & Riverwind** what the intercept orders reveal?
- How do they **protect Goldmoon and the Staff from Toede**, who is in Solace expecting a report that will never come?
- Who are **Toede's masters** ("people with sharper teeth") — and are they the same interest as **the buyer** / **X.T.**?
- The Seekers keep **priests, scholars, and Solamnic sympathizers** from the Staff — are those exactly the people who can identify it (e.g., Boilerdang, the party's Solamnic finds)?
