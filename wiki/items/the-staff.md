## What we know
- An item the buyer is fixated on: "the Staff must not reach X.T. The buyer said it twice." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Torn Map Scrap / Old Road](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-map-scrap.md))
- **[Goldmoon](../npcs/goldmoon.md) has the Staff.** A field order from **Fewmaster Toede** to the Solace roadblock crew states plainly: **"The woman has the staff"** — almost certainly *this* Staff. ([Seeker Field Order — Toede to Voss](../documents/seeker-field-order-toede.md)) (Session 03)
- It is being **actively hunted**: Toede set a targeted ambush to intercept Goldmoon for it, and considers her companion [Riverwind](../npcs/riverwind.md) "expendable." (Session 03)
- Goldmoon carries it **wrapped in cloth or leather**; the Seekers' orders are to **seize it intact.** ([intercept orders](../documents/seeker-field-order-toede.md)) (Session 03)
- Telling clue: the Seekers order their crews to **keep "local priests, scholars, or Solamnic sympathizers" away from the object** — i.e., the very people who could **recognize or use** it. This points to a **religious / scholarly / Solamnic** significance. ([intercept orders](../documents/seeker-field-order-toede.md)) (Session 03)
- **[Tika](../npcs/tika.md) believes the Staff is a religious item.** First local read on its nature — consistent with the Seekers barring priests/scholars/Solamnics from it. (Session 04)
- **It is a "blue staff as bright as a glacier."** Per [The Song of Riverwind](../documents/song-of-riverwind.md) (Session 04), the Staff is **blue and brilliant, ice-bright** — first description of its appearance.
- **It produces blue light and can spirit its bearer away.** The song recounts that when the Que-Shu tried to **stone Riverwind**, and Goldmoon joined him, **"the staff flares in blue light, and both of them vanish."** So the Staff can **emit blue light** and **make people vanish** (teleport / escape / protection) — at least under threat. (Session 04)
- **Riverwind found it on a quest "east and away," "to search for strong magic at the lip of the morning."** It was the object of a quest the Que-Shu chieftain set; Riverwind returned with it (and "darkness of stone in his eyes"). ([The Song of Riverwind](../documents/song-of-riverwind.md)) (Session 04)
- **It is a HEALING relic — a touch/strike of the Staff heals.** When [Hederick](../npcs/high-theocrat-hederick.md) fell face-first into the inn's fire, the [lute-wizard](../npcs/the-lute-wizard.md) handed the Staff to **[Boilerdang](../characters/boilerdang-criddlycrotch.md)**, who **struck Hederick with it and healed his burns.** Contact with the Staff mends injury. (Session 04)
- **Its power can be invoked by someone other than Goldmoon.** Boilerdang — a true cleric — wielded it to heal; it worked in his hands. (Whether it works for *anyone*, or only the worthy / the faithful, is untested.) (Session 04)
- **It was used openly** in a packed room during the door-to-door hunt for it — a public miracle, witnessed. Who now knows it was *the* Staff is an open question. (Session 04)
- **It left Goldmoon's hands unwillingly.** The [lute-wizard](../npcs/the-lute-wizard.md) produced and used it **without her consent** — the relic does not stay only with its bearer's agreement. (Session 04)

## Open questions
- What does the Staff *do* in full — it **heals by touch** (Session 04) and can **flare blue and make its bearer vanish** under threat (the song). Are these one power or several? Why is it worth a manhunt (and worth keeping from X.T.)?
- **Does it work for anyone, or only some?** It healed in Boilerdang's hands. Many holy relics answer only to the faithful or the pure — is that why the Seekers (false priests) want it but fear who can use it?
- **Where is "the lip of the morning"** Riverwind found it — a place, ruin, or being in the east? Tied to X.T., the buried relics, or a silenced god?
- A **blue religious staff** kept from priests of the "false" gods — is it a relic of **[Mishakal](../lore/mishakal.md)** or another silenced god? (See the confiscated Mishakal "false god token.")
- How did **Goldmoon** come to carry it — and does she know its full significance? (The song says it was Riverwind who brought it back.)
- Are **Toede's** masters ("people with sharper teeth") the same interest as **the buyer** / **X.T.**?
- Is it connected to the "relics" said to be buried near Solace, or to the recovered arcane schematic?
