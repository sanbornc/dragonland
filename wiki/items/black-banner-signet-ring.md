# Black Banner Signet Ring

An iron signet ring recovered from Captain Varek.

## What we know
- Iron, bearing a **black field marked by five claw-like slashes** — the first known insignia of the otherwise heraldry-less Black Banners. (`wiki/documents/captain-vareks-papers.md`)
- Described as an "unknown military insignia"; likely an officer's seal. (`wiki/documents/captain-vareks-papers.md`)

## Open questions
- What do the five claw-like slashes signify?
- Could it pass as Black Banner authority — useful for infiltration or forged orders?
