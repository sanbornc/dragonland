## What we know
- A **party-owned wagon** enchanted with **Bag of Holding–like** properties — it
  holds far more than its physical size should allow (extradimensional storage).
- **Convention:** any **unclaimed loot** defaults to the wagon. If an item isn't
  being carried by a specific character, assume it is stored here.

## Stored here by default (unclaimed loot)
- Ringmaster's Crimson Cane — [Ringmaster's Crimson Cane](ringmasters-crimson-cane.md)
- Shadow-Stained Cloak — [Shadow-Stained Cloak](shadow-stained-cloak.md)
- Calliope Shard — [Calliope Shard](calliope-shard.md)
- Ringmaster's Final Ticket — [Ringmaster's Final Ticket](ringmasters-final-ticket.md)
