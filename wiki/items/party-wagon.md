## What we know
- A **party-owned wagon** enchanted with **Bag of Holding–like** properties — it
  holds far more than its physical size should allow (extradimensional storage).
- **Convention:** any **unclaimed loot** defaults to the wagon. If an item isn't
  being carried by a specific character, assume it is stored here.

## Stored here by default (unclaimed loot)
- Ringmaster's Crimson Cane — `wiki/items/ringmasters-crimson-cane.md`
- Shadow-Stained Cloak — `wiki/items/shadow-stained-cloak.md`
- Calliope Shard — `wiki/items/calliope-shard.md`
- Ringmaster's Final Ticket — `wiki/items/ringmasters-final-ticket.md`

*(Claimed instead: Renob carries the magic bone dice. The Wolfcall Whistle is a
shared party item — `wiki/items/wolfcall-whistle.md`.)*
