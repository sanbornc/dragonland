# Oathkeeper's Blade

A rare longsword recovered from the buried archive in session 02; **carried by
Vaelor.** Possibly the Honor altar's return for the sword he offered there.

## What we know
- **Rare**, **+1 Longsword.** (session 02)
- **Once per long rest:** when an **ally within 30 feet fails a save against Fear, Charm, or being Frightened**, the wielder may let them **reroll.** (session 02)
