## What we know
- Single-use wondrous item — a red-and-black ticket that never fully stops
  fluttering. ([Session 01 — Crimson Circus Crossover (Bridge Session)](../../sessions/01-2026-06-02-crimson-circus-crossover.md))
- Reaction, when you fail a saving throw against being charmed, frightened,
  restrained, or magically pulled: tear the ticket to reroll the save (you must
  use the new result). (session 01)
- When used, the ticket burns away and whispers *"No refunds."* (session 01)
- Acquired during the bridge session; unclaimed, so stored in the **party
  wagon** by default ([Party Wagon](party-wagon.md)).
