## What we know
- The Ringmaster's personal ledger, recovered as loose pages from a locked wagon. ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Special Cargo / Things Not To Say](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-special-cargo.md))
- Records "special cargo," roads, payments, witnesses, and warnings; the Ringmaster would save it "before the masks" if the wagon burned. ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Back-Cover List](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-back-cover-list.md))
- The pages we hold are transcribed under `wiki/documents/`.
