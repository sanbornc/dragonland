## What we know
- Rare story item — a splinter of brass, bone, and black crystal taken from the
  **broken Calliope Mind**. ([Session 01 — Crimson Circus Crossover (Bridge Session)](../../sessions/01-2026-06-02-crimson-circus-crossover.md))
- Hums when lies are spoken nearby. (session 01)
- 1/day: cast *detect thoughts*, but only to sense surface emotions and intent,
  not full thoughts; afterward the holder hears circus music for 1 minute.
  (session 01)
- Acquired as Crimson Circus loot during the bridge session; unclaimed, so
  stored in the **party wagon** by default ([Party Wagon](party-wagon.md)).
