# The Seekers

## What we know

- A **recognized religious authority** in the world: false priests who claim
  authority but **cannot perform real miracles** (true divine power is believed
  long gone). — [Divine power in the world](../lore/divine-power.md) (GM background)
- They have an **armed presence at [Solace](../locations/solace.md)**: as the party
  entered, a **roadblock** stopped and searched them, manned by **goblins wearing
  Seeker symbols** alongside **another (non-goblin) person.** (Session 03)
- So the Seekers **command or employ goblins** — and **control who enters Solace.**
  (Session 03)

## Open questions

- Are the Seekers the **"new lies"** the Ringmaster's ledger sets against the
  "old gods"? (see [Miracle-bearers](../lore/miracle-bearers.md))
- Is **[High Theocrat Hederick](../npcs/high-theocrat-hederick.md)** a Seeker — and
  is he their leader at Solace? ("Theocrat" fits the Seekers' claimed authority.)
- Are these the **same goblins** that wounded the bears on the forest road
  (Session 03), and/or the **Black Banner–hired goblins** from the tower/archive
  (Session 02)?
- What are they searching for at the Solace roadblock — and how will they react to
  a true miracle-worker like [Boilerdang](../characters/boilerdang-criddlycrotch.md)?
