## What we know
- A site referred to only by the initials "X.T." — "do not speak the full name." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Torn Map Scrap / Old Road](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-map-scrap.md))
- Possibly an "old [site] below the green." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Torn Map Scrap / Old Road](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-map-scrap.md))
- The buyer insists "the Staff must not reach X.T." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Torn Map Scrap / Old Road](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-map-scrap.md))
- Marked on both the torn map scrap and the regional map, east of Haven and Solace. ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Torn Map Scrap / Old Road](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-map-scrap.md), [Burned Regional Map](../documents/regional-map-burned.md))
- The Staff that "must not reach X.T." is now known to be carried by **[Goldmoon](../npcs/goldmoon.md)** — and a rival interest (**[Fewmaster Toede](../npcs/fewmaster-toede.md)** and his masters) is hunting it. Whether Toede's side wants it *for* X.T. or *away from* it is unknown. (Session 03)

## Open questions
- What is X.T.'s full name, and why must it not be spoken?
- Why must the Staff be kept from it?
- Are **Toede's masters** ("people with sharper teeth") connected to X.T.?
