# Goblin Camp Finds

Documents and notes recovered from the goblin camp on the surface of the
excavation site (session 02). Together they confirm the Black Banners **hired
the goblin warband** as expendable diggers.

## Black Banner Pay Chit
Stamped:
> Squad Three
> Surface Security

(Matches the dead Black Banner scout's unit — see `wiki/locations/hall-of-oaths.md`.)

## Crude Goblin Map
A poorly drawn map showing:
> Watchtower · Goblin Camp · Breach Tunnel

The lower levels are labeled only:
> OLD PLACE

## Goblin Orders
Found in the goblin leader's tent, in terrible Common:
> DIG HERE
> FIND RECORDS
> DON'T BREAK RECORDS
> DON'T BURN RECORDS
> DON'T EAT RECORDS
> REPORT TO HUMAN CAPTAIN
