# Notice of Road Authority

Handed to the party at the [Seeker](../factions/the-seekers.md) roadblock entering
[Solace](../locations/solace.md) (Session 03) when they asked **by what authority**
they were being searched. Player-transcribed from the note read at the scene — no
image recovered.

## Transcription

> **NOTICE OF ROAD AUTHORITY**
>
> By order of the High Theocrat and lawful agents of regional protection, all
> travelers are subject to inspection.
>
> Surrender of the following is mandatory: unauthorized relics, false holy symbols,
> foreign magical implements, Solamnic arms or insignia, and items used in
> fraudulent healing.
>
> Harboring heretics, oath-cultists, foreign agitators, or known fugitives is
> punishable by seizure of goods, imprisonment, or transfer to higher authority.
>
> — **High Theocrat**

## What it tells us

- The **[Seekers](../factions/the-seekers.md)** style themselves "lawful agents of
  regional protection" and act on the authority of **the High Theocrat** — almost
  certainly **[Hederick](../npcs/high-theocrat-hederick.md)** himself.
- Their confiscation list reveals their targets: **unauthorized relics**,
  **false holy symbols**, **foreign magical implements**, **Solamnic arms or
  insignia**, and **items used in fraudulent healing**.
- They criminalize **harboring** "heretics, oath-cultists, foreign agitators, or
  known fugitives" — with penalties up to "transfer to higher authority."

## Why it matters to the party

- **[Boilerdang](../characters/boilerdang-criddlycrotch.md)** is a *true* healer in
  a world where the Seekers call real divine healing **"fraudulent healing"** and
  brand sacred items **"false holy symbols."** His holy symbol — and any open use of
  his power — is exactly what this checkpoint hunts. See
  [Divine power in the world](../lore/divine-power.md).
- The party carries **Solamnic** items (the [officer's compass](../items/solamnic-officers-compass.md)
  and the recovered order sigil) — now declared contraband. See
  [Knights of Solamnia](../factions/knights-of-solamnia.md).
- **"Oath-cultists"** could target [Vaelor](../characters/vaelor-grinward.md)
  (Oath of Devotion paladin); **"foreign agitators / fugitives"** could endanger
  the escorted [travelers](../npcs/the-two-travelers.md), including the Chieftain's
  daughter — and the party for harboring them.
