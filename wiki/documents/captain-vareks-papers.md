# Captain Varek's Papers

Recovered from **Captain Varek** and his Black Banner patrol, and posted by the
DM (Jeremy) to the "Crit Happens" group chat on 2026-06-12 as the encounter's
loot manifest. The journal, intelligence packet, memorandum, and personal note
are reproduced here as recovered in-world documents.

## Coin & valuables
- 85 gp in mixed regional coinage
- **Black Banner Signet Ring** — see [Black Banner Signet Ring](../items/black-banner-signet-ring.md)
- **Solamnic Officer's Compass** — see [Solamnic Officer's Compass](../items/solamnic-officers-compass.md)

## Military Field Journal
Most entries are routine patrol notes — weather, supply inventories, guard
schedules. The last complete entry:

> Survey Team Three requested immediate extraction.
> Commander denied request.
> Claims archive priority remains unchanged.
> Hearing reports of a "Marshal" below.
> Likely superstition.
> If even half of what they're saying is—

The entry ends abruptly.

## Intelligence Packet — marked "COMMAND EYES ONLY"
Several pages are missing or damaged.

**Page One — Patrol Objective:**
> Establish security perimeter around excavation site.
> Protect survey teams.
> Prevent unauthorized access.
> Recover surviving records.

**Page Two:**
> Reports indicate lower structures remain intact.
> Ancient military archives are believed to survive beneath the watch station.

**Page Three (partially burned):**
> ...sealed records...
> ...pre-Cataclysm military reports...
> ...maps and surveys...
> ...do not destroy original documents...

## Commander Memorandum — signed "S. Valane"
> Captain Varek,
> Your assignment remains unchanged.
> Hold the surface position.
> Permit no civilians, treasure hunters, or local authorities access to the site.
> Our survey teams have confirmed the existence of lower archive chambers.
> Initial reports suggest extensive damage but not total loss.
> Any surviving records must be secured immediately.
> If resistance is encountered, recover documents first.
> Lives are expendable.
> Records are not.
> We are not searching for wealth.
> We are not searching for relics.
> We are searching for knowledge.
> The old kingdoms buried more than treasure.
> — S. Valane

## Hidden Personal Note
Found tucked between the pages of the packet (unsigned — presumably Varek or a
patrol officer):

> I still think this is madness.
> We've sent three survey teams already.
> The first vanished.
> The second refused to return below.
> The third claims the dead are still patrolling.
> If command wants old records so badly, they can come dig for them themselves.
