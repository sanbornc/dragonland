# Black Banner Guard Orders

Standing orders for the Black Banner guards posted at the buried archive,
recovered by the party in session 02 as they moved past the Hall of Oaths toward
the lower archive. They corroborate and extend Commander S. Valane's directives
in [Captain Varek's Papers](captain-vareks-papers.md).

## Orders
> - Secure the lower entrance.
> - Recover all sealed archive records.
> - Preserve military ledgers, ruin surveys, and sealed correspondence when possible.
> - Avoid unnecessary conflict with local dead unless obstructed.
> - Report findings to command.

## Notes
- "Avoid unnecessary conflict with **local dead** unless obstructed" confirms the
  Black Banners knew of the patrolling undead and were told to work around them.
- "Report findings to **command**" points back up the chain to S. Valane.
