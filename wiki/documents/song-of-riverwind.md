# The Song of Riverwind

A ballad performed in the **[Inn of the Last Home](../locations/inn-of-the-last-home.md)**
in [Solace](../locations/solace.md) (Session 04). An **unnamed wizard** in the bar
produced a **lute** and asked **[Goldmoon](../npcs/goldmoon.md)** to sing; she sang
this song. **[High Theocrat Hederick](../npcs/high-theocrat-hederick.md)** listened
but **did not appear to make the connection** between the song and the woman before
him. The lyrics tell the story of Goldmoon, Riverwind, and the **blue [Staff](../items/the-staff.md)** —
and are the party's clearest account yet of where the Staff came from and what it does.

## What it tells us

- **Goldmoon and Riverwind are lovers** — she the chieftain's daughter ("the
  princess"), he "a poor man's son." Her father **opposed the match** and "makes long
  roads between them."
- **Riverwind was sent east on a quest** — "east and away / to search for strong magic
  at the lip of the morning." This reads as a test or bride-price set by the chieftain.
- **He returned changed, carrying the Staff** — "darkness of stone in his eyes," and
  "a **blue staff as bright as a glacier**." First description of the Staff's
  appearance: **blue, brilliant, ice-bright.**
- **The tribe rejected his claim** — the chieftain "makes mockery of Riverwind's
  claim" and "orders the people to **stone the young warrior**."
- **The Staff has power, and it activated under threat** — as the stoning began and
  Goldmoon joined Riverwind, "the **staff flares in blue light, and both of them
  vanish**." The Staff can produce blue light and apparently **teleported / spirited
  them away** from the mob.
- **It explains how they came to be on the road** — they vanished from the Que-Shu
  grasslands via the Staff and have been traveling since (eventually toward Solace,
  seeking Hederick).
- **Hederick didn't connect the song to Goldmoon** — despite the very on-the-nose
  lyrics, the High Theocrat running the staff-hunt did not realize the singer was his
  quarry. The party's (and Goldmoon's) cover held.

## Open questions

- **What is the "strong magic at the lip of the morning"** Riverwind found the Staff
  at — a place, a ruin, a being? Is it tied to [X.T.](../locations/x-t.md), the buried
  relics near Solace, or the broken gods?
- **What does the Staff actually do** beyond the blue flare and the escape — is the
  vanishing teleportation, planar travel, or protection?
- **Why "darkness of stone in his eyes"** — what did Riverwind see or endure on the
  quest?
- Is the **blue staff** the [Mishakal](../lore/mishakal.md) connection the confiscated
  "false god token" hinted at — a relic of a silenced god?
