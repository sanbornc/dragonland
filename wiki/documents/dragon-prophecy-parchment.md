# The Dragon Prophecy — Brittle Parchment

A brittle parchment recovered from the buried archive beneath the tower
(session 02). Transcribed faithfully:

> When the old faith is ash,
> When crowns break and oaths are hollow,
> The scaled children of forgotten fire shall rise.
> Iron will march beneath black banners.
> The sky shall remember wings.
> And the world shall learn again to fear dragons.
