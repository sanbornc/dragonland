# Seeker Intercept Orders — Solace Roadblock

Three orders recovered from the roadblock crew after the party wiped them out at
Solace (Session 03), all part of one **targeted operation** to intercept
**[Goldmoon](../npcs/goldmoon.md)** and **[Riverwind](../npcs/riverwind.md)** and
seize **[the Staff](../items/the-staff.md)**. Player-transcribed — no images.

## 1. Folded field order (signed, found in the lock box)

Addressed to **Voss** ([Pellan Voss](../npcs/pellan-voss.md)) and signed by **Fewmaster Toede**:

> Voss,
>
> Stop asking questions and watch the road. The woman has the staff. The big
> plainsman is expendable. If you miss them, I will explain your failure to people
> with sharper teeth than mine.
>
> — **Fewmaster Toede**

## 2. Crude note (on the head goblin)

> Staff woman. Tall man. Watch road. Tell Voss.

## 3. Standing checkpoint order (by the High Theocrat's office)

> Watch for a Que-Shu woman traveling with a tall plainsman. Woman may carry a
> staff wrapped in cloth or leather. Object is to be seized intact. Woman is to be
> detained alive. Male companion may be subdued by force if necessary.
>
> Report discovery to Fewmaster Toede in Solace. Do not allow local priests,
> scholars, or Solamnic sympathizers near the object.
>
> By authority of the High Theocrat's office.

## What it tells us

- The roadblock was a **targeted ambush**, not routine: it hunted a **Que-Shu woman**
  ([Goldmoon](../npcs/goldmoon.md)) and a **tall plainsman** ([Riverwind](../npcs/riverwind.md)).
- **Goldmoon is Que-Shu** — her father the Chieftain leads the **Que-Shu** people.
- The **[Staff](../items/the-staff.md)** is carried **wrapped in cloth or leather**;
  it is to be **seized intact**, and **Goldmoon detained alive** — Riverwind only
  "subdued by force if necessary" (Toede's note calls him "expendable").
- **[Fewmaster Toede](../npcs/fewmaster-toede.md) is in Solace**, the report-back
  point — and he answers to someone worse ("people with sharper teeth than mine").
- The whole operation runs **"by authority of the High Theocrat's office"** —
  confirming **[Hederick](../npcs/high-theocrat-hederick.md)** / the
  **[Seekers](../factions/the-seekers.md)** are behind the staff-hunt.
- Telling clue about the Staff: they want to **keep priests, scholars, and Solamnic
  sympathizers away from it** — those are exactly the people who could **recognize
  or use** it. That points to a **religious / scholarly / Solamnic** significance
  (see [Mishakal](../lore/mishakal.md), [Divine power](../lore/divine-power.md),
  [Knights of Solamnia](../factions/knights-of-solamnia.md)).

## Open questions

- What *is* the Staff, that priests and scholars must be kept from it?
- Does Goldmoon know the full reason she's hunted?
