# Mishakal

## What we know

- A **named god** in the world. The party recovered a **cheap wooden symbol of
  Mishakal** from the Seeker roadblock's lock box, **labeled "false god token."**
  (Session 03)
- The **[Seekers](../factions/the-seekers.md) explicitly reject Mishakal as false**
  — consistent with their stance that true divine power is gone and all claimed holy
  power is fraud. See [Divine power in the world](../lore/divine-power.md).

## Open questions

- Who worships Mishakal, and what is the god associated with? (The token was among
  *confiscated* holy items, so someone in the region venerates Mishakal.)
- Is Mishakal connected to **[Boilerdang](../characters/boilerdang-criddlycrotch.md)'s**
  real divine power — "a god whose name he is only beginning to recognize"?
- Any tie to **[Goldmoon](../npcs/goldmoon.md)** or to **[the Staff](../items/the-staff.md)** she carries?
- The party also holds a **Silver Holy Symbol of Paladine** (party inventory) —
  another named god. How do Mishakal, Paladine, and the "old gods" relate?
