## What we know
- A brittle parchment recovered from the buried archive foretells the **return of dragons** — transcribed in `wiki/documents/dragon-prophecy-parchment.md`. (session 02)
- Its signs read like the present age: **"the old faith is ash"** (collapsed religion), **"crowns break and oaths are hollow"** (failing kingdoms, broken oaths), and **"iron will march beneath black banners"** — explicitly naming the **Black Banners.** (session 02)
- The payoff: **"the scaled children of forgotten fire shall rise,"** **"the sky shall remember wings,"** **"the world shall learn again to fear dragons."** — the campaign's titular dragons returning. (session 02)

## Why it matters
- It ties the **Black Banners' war** to a draconic prophecy — their march may be a sign of, a cause of, or a servant to the dragons' return. (`wiki/factions/black-banners.md`)
- It frames the setting: a post-faith, post-Cataclysm world on the edge of a return of dragons.

## Open questions
- "The old faith" may be the worship of **Paladine** — a silver holy symbol of Paladine was recovered at the site. (session 02)
- Are the Black Banners trying to **fulfil** the prophecy, **prevent** it, or unknowingly **enacting** it?
- Who are the "scaled children of forgotten fire" — true dragons, draconians, or something else?
- Is the buried archive's hidden knowledge connected to the prophecy?
