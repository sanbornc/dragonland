## What we know
- **The Crimson Circus** was the party's previous mini-campaign; Dragonland
  follows directly from it, bridged by the 2026-06-02 session.
  ([Session 01 — Crimson Circus Crossover (Bridge Session)](../../sessions/01-2026-06-02-crimson-circus-crossover.md))
- Much of the party's circus-themed gear comes from this arc: the Ringmaster's
  Crimson Cane, the Shadow-Stained Cloak, the Calliope Shard, the Ringmaster's
  Final Ticket, and the Wolfcall Whistle. (session 01)
- The **Calliope Mind** was a (now broken) entity or device; the Calliope Shard
  is a splinter of it. (session 01)
- A strong **werewolf / lycanthropy** thread runs through the arc — the Wolfcall
  Whistle, the Bloodmoon Charm, and an antitoxin that guards against lycanthropy
  from bites. (session 01)
- The circus's **Ringmaster** is a recurring figure. The recovered "Ringmaster's
  Ledger" documents in Dragonland share this circus identity. (session 01,
  `wiki/documents/`)

## Open questions
- Is the Ledger's Ringmaster the same Ringmaster as the Crimson Circus?
  (Strongly implied by the shared circus theme, but not confirmed in-fiction.)
- What was the Calliope Mind, and why is it broken?
- How does the lycanthropy thread carry into Dragonland?
