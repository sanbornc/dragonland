## What we know
- War appears imminent; armies are massing eastward under black banners. ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Roads / Payments / Witnesses](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-roads-payments.md))
- A Queen's forces are expected to march. ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Back-Cover List](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-back-cover-list.md))
- Roads are unsafe — made so by neither king's men nor bandits. ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Roads / Payments / Witnesses](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-roads-payments.md))
- The Ringmaster times his smuggling to "before war," knowing scrutiny rises once "marching starts." ([Ringmaster's Ledger — Special Cargo / Things Not To Say](../documents/ringmasters-ledger-special-cargo.md))
- A recovered prophecy ties the war to a foretold **return of dragons**: "iron will march beneath black banners." ([The Dragon Prophecy](the-dragon-prophecy.md))
