## What we know
- **True divine power is believed to have vanished centuries ago.** Most people
  in the world think the gods no longer answer. (GM background — [Boilerdang](../characters/boilerdang-criddlycrotch.md))
- **Mundane healing still exists** — through herbs, skill, rest, potions, and
  rare arcane magic. What is missing is **true holy healing**: wounds closing
  through prayer, disease lifting by divine grace, life returning because a god
  *answered*. Most people have never seen it. (GM background)
- **Real miracles are dangerous.** Because true holy power is so rare, those who
  display it provoke extreme reactions — some would worship it, some would fear
  it, and some would call it fraud, witchcraft, or heresy. (GM background)
- **False priests and Seekers** still claim religious authority, but they cannot
  perform real miracles. The "Seekers" appear to be a recognized religious
  authority/order in the world. (GM background)
- A person whose prayers genuinely *answer* is, in the world's terms, a true
  **[miracle-bearer](miracle-bearers.md)** — which is exactly the rare and
  dangerous thing the [Ringmaster](../npcs/the-ringmaster.md) traffics as
  "special cargo." This connects the religious lore to the tension the ledger
  records between "old gods" and "new lies."
- **[Boilerdang Criddlycrotch](../characters/boilerdang-criddlycrotch.md)** is a
  living example: as the campaign begins his prayers stop feeling empty and his
  divine magic works — almost unheard of in this world. (GM background)
